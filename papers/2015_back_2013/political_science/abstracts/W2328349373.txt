The objective of the paper is to analyse the status of tribal education with literacy rate, gross enrolment ratio, dropout rates and Gender Parity Index in India.The analysis is based on secondary data of Census of India, 2011.The percentage of literacy of tribes was only 8.54 per cent in 1961 which has increased to 63.1 per cent in 2011.The gross enrolment ratio is higher in class I to V which is 137.2 for ST boys and 136.7 for ST girls.The Gender Parity Index for ST children is almost same as all categories of children except for class XI to XII.
