The Polish Mycological society (PMS) was established on 5 November 2011, in Łódź.The aims of the society are to develop, coordinate and support mycological studies, and to popularize mycology in its widest sense among the people in Poland, in cooperation with mycologists and mycological societies worldwide.As required by Polish law, the Society was registered (entry 00004 22681) on 23 June 2012 in the National Court Register of the City of Warsaw District Court.Contact information for the Society is as follows.
