The evolving fifth generation (5G) cellular wireless networks are envisioned\nto provide higher data rates, enhanced end-user quality-of-experience (QoE),\nreduced end-to-end latency, and lower energy consumption. This article presents\nseveral emerging technologies, which will enable and define the 5G mobile\ncommunications standards. The major research problems, which these new\ntechnologies breed, as well as the measurement and test challenges for 5G\nsystems are also highlighted.\n
