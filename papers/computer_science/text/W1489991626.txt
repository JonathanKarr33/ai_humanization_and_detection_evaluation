Tables of Integral Transforms
In this chapter we provide a set of short tables of integral transforms of the func-
tions that are either cited in the text or in most common use in mathematical,
physical, and engineering applications. For exhaustive lists of integral transforms,
the reader is referred to Erdélyi et al. (1954), Campbell and Foster (1948), Ditkin
and Prudnikov (1965), Doetsch (1970), Marichev (1983), Debnath (1995), and
Oberhettinger (1972).
