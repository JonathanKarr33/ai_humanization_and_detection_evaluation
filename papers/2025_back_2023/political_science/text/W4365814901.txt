ChatGPT and the Future of Public Policy Analysis: Potential and Pitfalls of using ChatGPT in 
Policymaking 
 
Ridwan Islam Sifat1 
1School of Public Policy, University of Maryland, Baltimore County, Baltimore, MD 21250, USA 
 
 
 
 
 
rsifat1@umbc.edu 
 
Abstract 
 
Scholars increasingly rely on new artificial intelligence models for convenience and simple access 
to necessities due to the rapid evolution of scientific literature and technology. The invention of 
ChatGPT by OpenAI stands out as a key example of how significant advances in large language 
model (LLM) technology have recently changed the field of artificial intelligence (AI). Since 
ChatGPT's development, it has been tested by multiple sectors on various topics to see how well 
it functions in a natural and conversational mode. The crucial question is how much ChatGPT can 
influence public policy analysis. In this article, the researcher briefly explains ChatGPT's potential 
and the difficulties that users, such as researchers or policymakers, may continue to face. 
Keywords: ChatGPT; artificial intelligence; public policy; policymaking 
 
Introduction 
 
ChatGPT is an exceptionally advanced conversational artificial intelligence interface 
that has revolutionized how people interact with technology [6]. It uses Natural Language 

Processing (NLP) techniques to create a highly realistic and engaging user experience, 
allowing them to interact with ChatGPT as they would a human. What sets ChatGPT apart 
from other chatbots or virtual assistants is its ability to answer questions and provide a brief 
answer by acknowledging and challenging wrong assumptions or assumptions made by the 
user [3].  When asked a query, ChatGPT will automatically generate an explanation based on 
thousands of online resources, frequently without the user's further interaction [4]. As a result, 
it has been claimed that people have used ChatGPT to create academic papers and essays, and 
the system can provide supporting references upon request. Still, researchers claimed that the 
references are not corrected [1,5].  
 
The evolution of artificial intelligence technology has made it a robust platform with 
enormous potential to improve public policy analysis. Artificial intelligence systems are regarded 
as essential tools in policymaking due to their advanced skills in analyzing large datasets and 
identifying patterns. Policymakers can decide more accurately and precisely by using data-based 
insights rather than intuition or assumptions to inform their decisions [7]. ChatGPT stands out 
among the new AI-related technologies as promising and can alter how we conduct public policy 
analysis thoroughly. Natural language processing's unique ability for communication enhances 
channels of exchange between humans and machines [2]. It offers crucial input into intricate 
decision-making procedures at all tiers of international government agencies. But it is difficult to 
claim that employing ChatGPT will have a significant positive or negative impact on our society's 
governance, both in terms of efficiency improvements and the development of transparency. 
Positive Impact of Using ChatGPT in Public Policy  

 
ChatGPT is a highly sophisticated and advanced natural language processing tool 
capable of supporting public policy analysis and formulation. Its versatility makes it a beneficial 
resource for policymakers who want to navigate the complexities of modern governance more 
easily. While considering the benefits of incorporating ChatGPT in this particular context, there 
are numerous benefits to be acknowledged: 
 
1. With its advanced language processing capabilities, ChatGPT can delve deep into 
complex linguistic structures and often discover too subtle patterns for humans to 
understand. This makes it an essential tool for dissecting complex public debates 
surrounding policy issues, as it can accurately identify recurring themes and subtle 
arguments that might otherwise go unnoticed by people. ChatGPT allows 
policymakers to make informed decisions based on a thorough understanding of all 
sides of the issue by providing a comprehensive analysis of different perspectives on 
a given issue. 
2. ChatGPT is an exceptional tool capable of analyzing large amounts of unstructured 
data with great accuracy and precision. Its extraordinary analytical capabilities allow 
it to extract essential insights from large amounts of data, which can provide valuable 
inputs for policymaking in various domains. Moreover, using ChatGPT's advanced 
analytical functionality, policymakers can save considerable time and resources 
compared to manual data analysis methods. 
3. ChatGPT, an advanced platform with predictive modeling capabilities, empowers its 
users to create and predict the impact of policy changes in a comprehensive and 
insightful manner. Using its sophisticated algorithms and data analytics tools, 

ChatGPT enables policymakers and analysts to evaluate complex policy proposals 
by predicting their potential consequences on various societal aspects. Such depth of 
analysis can lead to informed decision-making that considers all possible outcomes 
while minimizing unintended negative impacts or risks associated with new policies. 
4. Policymakers can be facilitated to access insights and perform analysis. This implies 
that they can acquire important information at any moment without being constrained 
by the availability constraints posed by human analysts. ChatGPT's accessibility 
helps policymakers be more agile in their decision-making processes, as they can 
immediately access data-driven insights whenever needed. 
5. By offering a comprehensive and multidimensional approach, data analysis can 
effectively reduce bias in policymaking. With its ability to uncover blind spots or 
unconscious biases inherent in the decision-making process, this approach is 
essential in promoting equity and fairness in policy implementation. 
 
Potential Cons of Using ChatGPT 
 
Although ChatGPT has tremendous potential to help in various domains, it is essential to 
note some shortcomings that may hinder its use in public policy analysis and policymaking. 
 
1. The use of ChatGPT in policymaking has raised concerns about the level of transparency 
in the decision-making process. This is mainly due to the complexity of the algorithm 
employed by ChatGPT, which humans need help understanding. Identifying and 
understanding all the factors that affect the outputs generated through this platform can be 

particularly challenging, resulting in a lack of transparency or visibility into the results 
produced by ChatGPT. The absence of transparency that arises here poses significant 
challenges for policymakers who may struggle to trust these findings and make decisions 
based on them with confidence. 
2. The use of ChatGPT in public policy analysis raises significant ethical concerns. The 
potential consequences of perpetuating bias and promoting injustice cannot be 
overstated. This can lead to discriminatory practices and policies that undermine social 
justice efforts. Furthermore, the complexity of analyzing large datasets makes it 
difficult to detect hidden or inherent biases in the training data used by ChatGPT. Thus, 
if such biases are not detected during analysis, they may influence decision-making 
processes without being noticed. 
3. ChatGPT, although equipped to respond to a wide range of inputs, is bound by 
limitations set by its training data. This means that ChatGPT's ability to generate 
responses may be hampered if the input data it receives is limited or biased. As a result, 
there is a possibility that the outputs generated by ChatGPT may not accurately reflect 
the sentiments and views of the larger population on the issues at hand. As a result, it 
may formulate policy decisions without considering the needs and perspectives of all 
stakeholders, which may have potentially harmful consequences. 
4. ChatGPT's functionality depends primarily on the text input it receives. However, due 
to its limited ability to understand the overall context surrounding a policy issue, there 
may be instances where the responses it generates may not be adequately suited to 
address specific issues or policy-related situations. This lack of understanding can 

further lead to inadequate data collection and analysis, leading to uninformed policy 
decisions with potentially serious repercussions. 
 
Conclusion 
 
In conclusion, ChatGPT technology has the potential to revolutionize public policy 
analysis by improving communication between humans and machines. It can provide valuable 
input into complex decision-making processes at all levels of government agencies 
worldwide, thereby increasing efficiency and transparency in administration. Despite its 
advantages, policymakers and researchers must consider several challenges when using this 
emerging technology.
