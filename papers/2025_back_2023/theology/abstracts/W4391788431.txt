The Qosidah of the 34th Congress is a syi'iran written by DR. (H.C) KH. Afifudin Muhajir's&#x0D; intention was none other than Mahabbah's love for Nahdlatul Ulama, with the hope that it would&#x0D; be a means of gaining the approval of Al&#x0D; lah SWT. Therefore, the author's aim in conducting this&#x0D; research is to describe the da'wah message contained in the muktamar qosidah by DR. (H.C) KH.&#x0D; Afifuddin Muhajir. The research method used in this research is library research or what is usually&#x0D; called&#x0D; the Hermeneutics method. This method focuses on the activity of interpreting or meaning&#x0D; the text contained in the research object being studied. The conclusion and results of this research&#x0D; are that the da'wah messages studied by researchers are that there&#x0D; are three categories of da'wah&#x0D; messages, namely creed messages, sharia messages and moral messages. Of the three messages&#x0D; that researchers can express in the qosidah congress, there are three types of aqidah messages,&#x0D; namely faith in Allah, Allah's Messen&#x0D; ger and faith in the qodlo and qadar of Allah SWT.&#x0D; Meanwhile, there are two sharia messages contained in the qosidah, namely the sharia message in&#x0D; the form of worship, namely prayer and the sharia message in the form of deliberation. Finally, the&#x0D; moral mes&#x0D; sage contained in the qosidah is morals towards Allah, the Messenger of Allah and&#x0D; morals towards humans which are divided into three, namely morals towards oneself, towards&#x0D; family and towards other people.
