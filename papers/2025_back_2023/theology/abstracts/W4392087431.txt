This research is a descriptive quantitative research, in this research the researcher uses two variables, namely religious culture (X) and student morals (Y). This research was conducted by distributing questionnaires to students of MTs esa nusa islamic school housing binong kec.curug kab.Tangerang, Adapu subjects in this study were all students at MTs Esa Nusa Islamic School Binong totaling 74 students. The validity of the data uses the triangulation method, the analysis uses descriptive statistical analysis SPSS persi 34. The results show that there is a significant influence between the influence of religious culture on the morals of students at MTs esa nusa Islamic school binong, which is in accordance with the coefficient of determination test (R2) the influence of religious culture on student morals by 14.7% while the rest is influenced by other variables.
