The values of the European Union are important elements of this organisation’s contact with the international environment. Important political goals of the EU, which fall within the sphere of long-term objectives of its foreign policy, are developing ‘rulesbased multilateralism’ and contributing to the creation of ‘rules and values-based international order’. The aim of this article is to reconstruct and present in a concise and approachable way the place of European values in an international domain, in the EU’s external activity directed at the creation of the title international phenomenon.
